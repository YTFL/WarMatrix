# WarMatrix: Premium Feature Additions

This document outlines planned "WOW" features to elevate the WarMatrix tactical simulation suite to a premium, state-of-the-art interactive experience.

## ✅ 1. Neural Voice Synthesis (Radio Uplink) - COMPLETED
- **Status**: Operational.
- **Features**: Military high-pass filtering, browser-native TTS, toggleable via console.

## ⛈️ 2. Dynamic Weather Visual Overlays
- **Concept**: Animated particle systems on the `TacticalMapDisplay`. Weather impacts movement speed and sensor ranges.

## 🔊 3. Contextual Soundscape Engine
- **Concept**: A procedural audio engine that adjusts ambient sound (wind, machinery, artillery) based on the battle intensity.

## 🗺️ 4. Strategic "Heatmap" Analytics
- **Concept**: A toggleable visual layer showing cumulative casualty rates and fire intensity hotspots over the course of a turn.

## ✅ 5. SOP Tactical Handbook (Doctrine Center) - COMPLETED
- **Status**: Operational.
- **Features**: Full-screen Doctrine terminal, searchable SOPs, operational requirement briefs.

## 🧱 6. Relational Logistics Integration
- **Concept**: Linking tactical losses directly to the MSSQL Economic/Resource database. Permanent asset degradation.

## 🛰️ 7. Satellite Surveillance (Drone Feed PIP)
- **Concept**: A Picture-in-Picture window showing a "Thermal" or "Night Vision" flyover of selected grid sectors.

## 🗃️ 8. Tactical Asset Vault (3D Viewer)
- **Concept**: A high-fidelity 3D model viewer to inspect hardware in detail before deployment.

## 📽️ 9. Cinematic Combat Replay
- **Concept**: Replay the last turn's movement/combat using an intelligent "Movie Camera" follow-cam system.

## 🕵️ 10. AI Espionage (Deep Cover Intel)
- **Concept**: Deploy AI units to "infiltrate" enemy nodes. Success reveals hidden enemy attributes in the Threat Engine.

## 🗣️ 11. AI Personality Cores
- **Concept**: Switch the AI Strategist's "Mind Core." 
  - *Core A (VANGUARD)*: Aggressive, high-risk maneuvers.
  - *Core B (BULWARK)*: Defensive, fortification-focused.
  - *Core C (SPECTER)*: Guerrilla tactics, stealth, and sabotage.

## 📡 12. Electronic Warfare (EM Jamming)
- **Concept**: Visual "Static" circles on the map showing where enemy jammers are active. Radar becomes "fuzzy" or disabled in these zones.

## ⛓️ 13. Supply Chain Logistics Graph
- **Concept**: A line-based visualization connecting HQ to front-line units. If the line is cut by an enemy unit, the unit enters "Attrition Mode" (low ammo/repair).

## 🪖 14. Unit Veteran Status (EXP System)
- **Concept**: Individual units (e.g., Tank-001) gain EXP for kills. They gain "Veteran" status, improved stats, and can be custom-named by the Commander.
